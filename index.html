Hello, I’m Sydney.

Speech-Language Pathology graduate student · Aphasia & literacy research assistant.

My goal: To support individuals with communication disorders through evidence-based, person-centered, and creatively informed care.

I’m a graduate student in Speech-Language Pathology with a passion for aphasia, literacy, and neurogenic communication disorders. My background in creative leadership strengthens my ability to communicate, collaborate, and approach clinical challenges with innovation and empathy. I currently work as a research assistant on a mixed-methods study exploring how people with aphasia experience and feel about reading.

  • Aphasia & acquired neurogenic communication disorders
  • Reading and literacy in adults with neurological conditions
  • Evidence-based, person-centered clinical care
  • Creative approaches to communication and rehabilitation
